We are caretakers of the environment

A Tuatara Strucutre is built forever, without harm to the environment it will occupy. We are committed to ensuring our practices have low carbon footprint and that our materials are from sustainable sources and able to be recycled without damage to future generations.

Swing-Leg Roof Lift construction

We are one of the first New Zealand companies to construct using Swing leg roof lift construction. This is important both for construction worker health and safety, but also allows us to deliver the project faster. The building's roof is constructed first and built entirely on the ground - eliminating the need for scaffolding assembly. Once complete, the building is lifted into place using massive cranes that are positioned around the building.

The building is lifted in a virtual symphony of choreography that takes place over the course of a few hours. As the building is lifted the steel legs are swung into place and then secured, and the legs are removed. A process that can take several weeks to get to a roofing stage on a building traditionally, is completed over a day and the building can be made water tight within a week.
